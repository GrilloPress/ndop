7. Register your preference

Setting a decision

You have the right to request that your personally identifiable information is not used or shared beyond your own direct care. Using this service will confirm your decision and ensure your preferences do not change.

If you opt-out of sharing your data, this will be respected by all organisations that use health and social care information. This does not include if there is a legal reason or obligation to use your information.

Your decision will not affect your own direct care in any way. You can also change your mind and set a different preference in the future, if you wish to do so.
