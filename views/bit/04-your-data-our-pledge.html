4. Your data - our pledge

When using your data for improving health and care services, we pledge to:

  • Use anonymised data wherever we can
  • Use your personally identifiable data to benefit health and care
  • Use the minimum amount of information where possible e.g. removing your name
  • Give you a choice over sharing your personally identifiable data wherever we can
  • Never use your data for marketing or insurance purposes - unless you specifically request it
  • Respect your decision if you opt-out of sharing your personally identifiable data beyond your individual care
  • Only share your information where allowed by law
  • Only share your information where there has been independent scrutiny and approval
  • Make it clear what we use your data for, who we share it with and the reasons why
  • Take every step to keep your data secure at all times
