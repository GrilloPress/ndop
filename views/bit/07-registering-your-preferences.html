7. Registering your preferences

Setting a decision

You have the right to request that your personally identifiable information is not used or shared beyond your own individual care. Using this service will confirm your decision and ensure your preferences do not change.

If you opt-out of sharing your data, this will be respected by NHS Digital which collects, processes and releases health and care data on a national basis. It will also be respected by all other organisations that use health and social care information by March 2020.

Your decision will not affect your own individual care in any way. You can also change your mind and set a different preference in the future, if you wish to do so.
